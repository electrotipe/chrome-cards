Easily learn new languages and facts.

Answer interactive flash cards from the comfort of your browser! Either use the Chrome extensions for an icon on your toolbar or use them full-screen from this site.

Pick from three different modes of question answering to suit your knowledge level: multiple-choice, type-in, or type-in with autocomplete.

As you answer the cards, the system will track what you get right and wrong, and show you the cards that you need to practice more.

You can check your stats to see how you're doing with the deck. The stats are based the "Leitner system", which put cards in different stacks depending on how much you need to practice them.
